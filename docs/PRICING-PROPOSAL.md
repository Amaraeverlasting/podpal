## 1. RECOMMENDED TIERS

### TIER 1 — SOLO | $29/month
**Target:** Independent podcasters, 1-2 hosts, 2-4 episodes/month
- Up to 8 hours live session time/month
- Real-time topic detection + research cards
- Fact-check alerts
- Guest intel loading (pre-episode brief)
- Up to 3 follow-up question suggestions per topic
- 1 user seat / Web app only

### TIER 2 — PRO | $79/month
**Target:** Serious independents, journalists, small teams
- Up to 30 hours live session time/month
- Everything in Solo
- Unlimited follow-up suggestions
- Priority research queue (faster card delivery)
- 3 user seats
- Episode history + research archive (90 days)
- Export session research as PDF

### TIER 3 — NETWORK | $299/month
**Target:** Podcast networks, corporate podcast teams, media companies
- Up to 120 hours live session time/month (pooled)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 15 user seats
- Dedicated guest intel profiles (CRM-style)
- Admin dashboard + usage analytics
- Priority support (24h response)
- Custom topic domains

### TIER 4 — ENTERPRISE | Custom (~$800+/month)
**Target:** Large media houses, broadcast networks, newsrooms
- Unlimited hours + seats
- SSO/SAML, SLA, dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations, on-premise option

**Risk 1: Deepgram costs erode margin if usage exceeds estimates**
Mitigation: Hard soft caps + overage pricing ($0.40/hr Solo, $0.35/hr Pro). Monitor P99 usage in beta. Volume contract with Deepgram at 500 users.

**Risk 2: $29 Solo perceived as too cheap, undermining premium positioning**
Mitigation: Lead with Pro ($79) in all marketing. Consider raising Solo to $39 at Month 3. Beta data will show willingness-to-pay before committing.

**Risk 3: Riverside/Otter ships real-time feature within 12 months**
Mitigation: Lock Network/Enterprise early (sticky accounts). Build data gravity via guest profiles, research archive, episode history. Invest in research quality over speed.
